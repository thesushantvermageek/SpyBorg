About The Idea

A SpyBorg FM transmitter is a low-power FM radio transmitter that broadcasts a signal from a portable audio device (such as an MP3 player) to a standard FM Radio. Typically they broadcast on any FM frequency from 87.5 Mhz to 108.0 Mhz in most of the world

Components

Components used in building an FM Radio Transmitter.

Design

Basic Design of an FM radio transmitter. It can be divided in three parts.

9V FM Transmitter

Audio Amplification, oscillation, RF Amplification of the input wave happens here. It is the main body of the SpyBorg

Electret Microphone

It is a permanently charged Di-Electric.The Diaphragm of the microphone acts as one of the plates in the capacitor.

Aerial/Antenna

This is the component that converts the electronic FM signal into electromagnetic waves, which are then transmitted. A 22 measure cooper wire is suitable for antenna.
